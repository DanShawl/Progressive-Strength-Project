An All Encompassing Approach

Injury-Specific Training Programs

Clients are given custom programs designed around their post-physical therapy injury and/or injury-prevention goals.

Hybrid and Buddy Sessions

Committing can be tough, so both hybrid (virtual/in-person) and buddy sessions are more than welcome.

App Tracking

Clients will have access to a top-of-the-line application that will help them keep track of their workouts and progress.

Guided Nutrition

Nutrition is a key component that clients often find difficult. Guided nutrition is offered to help lead you in the right direction.

How it Works

You can get started towards your injury-free lifestyle in three steps.

Free phone consultation

First, you'll set up a free consultation via email. During the consultation, we'll discuss your goals, any past injuries, and what types of fitness you enjoy.

Free training session

After your consultation, you'll get a free training session and assessment to get a taste of what you would be doing going forward.

Continue your program

Lastly, you just need to be consistent and keep working towards your goals. We're in this together and I'll be there with you every step of the way.

Find the answers you need

How long will it take to see results?

Results are relative - we'll be making realistic short-term and long-term goals that will help keep motivation high. Consistency will yield results in time.

What type of program should I choose?

Whatever your life allows with the least amount of resistance. Virtual and buddy sessions are offered for that exact reason.

How do I get started?

Shoot me an email to set up a free phone consultation where we can put together a plan for your injury-free life.
